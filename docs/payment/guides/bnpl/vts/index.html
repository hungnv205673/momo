FAQs​

1. Benefits from being a partner of Pay Later

- Boost your conversion rate. Window shoppers are now empowered to try out your products - Increase your average order value. Pay Later users tend to spend +60% than before - Acquire new customer from more than 32 million MoMo users - Boost your brand awareness and lots of co-brand marketing promotion with MoMo And many more!

2. Is Pay Later safe and secured?

MoMo E-Wallet is licensed and authorized by the State Bank, is the strategic partners of most of the largest banks in Vietnam. MoMo meets international security standards: PCI DSS highest level; 2FA, SSL/TLS, Tokenization. Multi-layer security when logging in & making payment: OTP authentication code, fingerprint / face authentication, automatically lock the application and automatically prevent transactions with any sign of fraud. Therefore, Pay Later by MoMo strictly follows the general safety and security criteria.

3. How to be a partner of Pay Later?

Our goal is to provide customers with the best pay later/pay in instalment in varies of industry, such as: Apparel & Fashion, Cosmetic, Accessories, Education, Travel, etc. We are always willing partner up. - Please contact us via hotline: 1900 636 652 (Press 2 for English) - Email: merchant.care@momo.vn

4. How to give customer a refund?

Whether customers are able to refund, it depends on your policy. If yes, you can config via MoMo for Business (M4B), just choose the transaction, properties and choose refund.

5. Where can I find the payment reconciliation?

You can track and manage your transactions online via MoMo for Business (M4B). Transactions are updated real time and are defined as each payment method (MoMo E-Wallet, Pay Later, Flik).
